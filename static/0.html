Author name:Harper Lee

Summary:

Scout Finch lives with her brother Jem, and their widowed father, Atticus, in the sleepy Alabama town of Maycomb. ... Scout goes to school for the first time that fall and detests it. She and Jem find gifts apparently left for them in a knothole of a tree on the Radley property.
